About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. He has a deep passion for capturing the natural beauty of Scotland's landscapes, wildlife, and coastal scenes. With years of experience and expertise, Malcolm's photographs reflect the raw and rugged beauty of the Scottish wilderness.

In addition to landscape and wildlife photography, Malcolm also specializes in capturing special moments at weddings, portraits, and other events. His keen eye for detail and creative approach ensure that each photograph tells a unique story.

Through his photography, Malcolm aims to inspire others to appreciate and preserve the natural world. His work has been featured in various publications and exhibitions, earning him recognition as one of Scotland's top photographers.
